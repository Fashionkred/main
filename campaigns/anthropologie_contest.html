HOW TO ENTER

NO PURCHASE OR PAYMENT OF ANY KIND IS NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN.

You can access the contest through the Cult Collection web site at www.startcult.com. Participants entering the contest must follow these steps:
  • Create a look on Cult Collection using at least one Anthropologie item.
  • Tag that look with the hashtag “#Anthropologie”.
  • Share that look on either Instagram and/or Pinterest and include the hashtags “#Anthropologie” and “#CultCollection” in the share message.
Each look thats fulfills these criteria constitutes an eligible entry into the contest. Participants may enter as many times as they wish. No responsibility can be accepted for entries not received for whatever reason.


WINNER SELECTION

Three distinct winners will be chosen from all eligible entries on the basis of the number of "Steals" received by each look on Cult Collection. Entrants will be ranked by the number of steals received by each eligible individual look they have created (i.e. NOT the total number of steals received by all eligible looks they have created), and the top three distinct entrants will be declared winners.

The winners will be announced on or about September 26th, 2015 on or about 4PM PST. Announcement and instructions for prize will be posted on Cult Collection’s social channels on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Pinterest. Each entrant is responsible for monitoring his/her Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, or Pinterest account for prize notification and receipt or other communications related to this contest. If a potential prize winner cannot be reached by Administrator (or Sponsor) within fifteen (15) days, using the contact information provided at the time of entry, or if the prize is returned as undeliverable, that potential prize winner shall forfeit the prize. Upon the request of the Sponsor, the potential winner may be required to return an Affidavit of Eligibility, Release and Prize Acceptance Form and IRS W-9 form. If a potential winner fails to comply with these official rules, that potential winner will be disqualified. Prizes may not be awarded if an insufficient number of eligible entries are received.
